## Routine

It is to get a sense of what each unit is doing and thus facilitate the efficient allocation of responsibilities. Therefore, active participation is crucial unless you struggle with urgent tasks.

### <u>Weekly meeting</u>

- The weekly meeting is at 11:00 KST every Thursday.
- Each team member writes down progress of the previous week and goals of the next week in the corresponding meeting log.
- Regarding progress, sharing links of the tangible outcomes is recommended; even if they are  not finished yet, it is ok.
- Unit leaders briefly explain the goals and progress of their units.
- Every team member can share their opinions or worries.

### <u>Tokamak Network meeting</u>

- The Tokamak Network meeting is at 11:00 KST on Friday of the last week every month.
- We have to show the progress of the existing month and goals of the next month.
- Kevin assesses the progress of the project and give advice, if needed.
- Regarding sharing the progress, it is highly recommended to use tangible materials.
- For example, if you do research on layer 2 protocols, it is possible to present the results in powerpoint.
- You can share progress, relevant links, and goals [here](/626b872f3ba64d1598899ba2cb8d25e6).
