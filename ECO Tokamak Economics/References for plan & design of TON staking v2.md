## Value propositions of TON staking v2 (WIP)

**Q. Why should users choose Tokamak Network over other competitors?
**

A. Compared to major L2 services like Arbitrum or Optimism, we offer following merits:
1) A part of newly minted TON (TON seigniorage) will compensate L2 growth and security.
1-1) More specifically, the larger a specific L2 is, the more newly minted TON it gets; since TON seigniorage for sequencers will accrue to the sequencer collateral, it reinforces L2 security.
2) We offer TON staking-based native fast withdrawal service.
