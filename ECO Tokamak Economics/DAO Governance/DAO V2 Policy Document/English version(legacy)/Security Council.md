# The role of the Security Council

The main role of the Security Council is to manage and mitigate critical risks within the Tokamak ecosystem. This council is tasked with making timely and emergency response decisions to safeguard the interests of the DAO, its members, and the wider community. Currently, the existing DAO committee will serve as the Security Council. In the future, we plan to hold elections to expand the council with additional members from the community.

# **Signing Requirements**

Currently, a proposal can advance to the next or final stage of execution only after receiving approval from the Security Council. A 2/3 majority vote is required for a proposal to pass the Security Council's review
