There are two types of votes in the Tokamak DAO v2:

1. **Temperature check (Snapshot):** This is a preliminary vote that gauges the community's interest in a proposal before it moves on to the next stage. 
1. **On-chain vote:** If the proposal passes the temperature check, it will move on to an on-chain vote facilitated by **Tally.**

The eligibility threshold for proposal creation and the number of votes required for a proposal to pass at each stage will be covered in the "Lifecycle of a Proposal" section.
