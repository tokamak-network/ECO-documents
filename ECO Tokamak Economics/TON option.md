## Rationale

TON, like other alt-coins, is quite volatile. At the same time, derivatives, including futures or options, do not exist to mitigate such volatilities. Therefore, the demand for TON can dramatically increase if we can offer ‘hedging instruments.’

## General idea

Users bet money on how the TON price will change in the future. For example, while some predict the TON price would fall below 2,500 KRW, others can expect the opposite. The group that makes the right forecast will take the bigger share of the whole proceeds.

## Example

Let’s say the current TON price is 2,500 KRW and each user makes their bets on how the TON price will change in 3 months. For simplicity, we assume both the TON optimists and pessimists invest 100 TON respectively. After 3 months, the TON price is up by 20% and thus becomes 3,000 KRW. Consequently, 20% of the investment by the TON pessimists goes to the TON optimists:
TON optimists: 100 TON + 100 TON * 0.2 = 120 TON
TON pessimists: 100 TON - 100 TON * 0.2 = 80 TON

When it comes to the individual distribution, it is proportional to the investment. If the TON optimist Bob initially locks 3 TON, 3% of the 100 TON locked by the TON optimists, 3% * 120 TON = 3.6 TON will be for Bob.
