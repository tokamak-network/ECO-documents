Tokamak Network is transitioning its existing Tokamak Staking service towards a community-driven model to align with our vision of decentralization and community-centric ecosystems. Previously managed directly by Tokamak Network, the staking service will now be hosted and operated by community members.

---

## 🌱 Background and Conditions of Transition

- The community version is fundamentally the same service as the existing staking service, with the key difference being its independence from backend infrastructure (such as subgraphs, APIs, etc.).
- Initially, Tokamak Network will officially host the community version, providing users sufficient time to familiarize themselves.
- After a certain period following the closure of official hosting, the service will transition fully to community or individual user-hosting, including local environments.

---

## 📅 Staged Restriction Timeline

To minimize confusion and ensure a smooth transition, the following schedule will be implemented:

|  | Content | Notice |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1st week | Open Community version (hosting both version) | 1 blog + 1 x posting |
| 2nd week | Restriction of unstake and withdraw button(only available at community version) | x posting |
| 3th week | Restriction of stake button | x posting |
| 4th week | Shutdown the simple staking | x posting  + documentation |
| 6th week | Shutdown hosted community version | x posting |

- Announcements to the community via X at each stage.
- In the first week, just open the community version and add a banner or link to the community version in the simple staking service
- Before opening the community version, a blog post about introducing the community version will be published
- After the restriction of the staking button in the simple staking, a warning message or tooltip will be added for the staking button. (It could be disabled or linked to the community version)
- Same as other actions(update seigniorage, unstake, withdraw)
- When shutting down the current service, the documentation which includes the etherscan guide will be opened.
- After the shutdown of simple staking, only the community version will be hosted for about 2 weeks
