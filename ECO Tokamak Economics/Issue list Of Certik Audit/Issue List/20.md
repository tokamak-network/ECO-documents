# **LAY-02 | `OperatorManagerV1_1` and `OperatorManagerProxy` Storage Layout Inconsistency**

| **카테고리** | **심각도** | **위치** | **상태** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Coding-issue, Volatile-code** | **중간** | **OperatorManagerProxy.sol#L11-L11: L11-L11OperatorManagerV1_1.sol#L45-L45: L45-L45** | **Pending** |

### **Description**

The `OperatorManagerProxy` contract inherits `Proxy, ERC1967Upgrade, Ownable` contracts and declare another variable `address public rollupConfig`.

The `OperatorManagerV1_1` contracts inherits `Ownable, OperatorManagerStorage` and the `OperatorManagerStorage` contract declares its custom storage variables.

These two storage layouts are not consistent because the `Proxy` contract also contains its own storage variables, as it inherits `ProxyStorage`. The storage layout inconsistencies between the proxy contract and logic contract could cause the contract to malfunction.

### **Recommendation**

Ensure that the storage layout are consistent between all proxy contracts and their respective logic contracts.

---

## Feedback


OperatorManagerProxy’s inheried`Proxy` contract  is the @openzeppelin/contracts/proxy/Proxy.sol which has no storage.
