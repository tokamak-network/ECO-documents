# <u>2022</u>

## Goal

Establish the theoretical foundation for Tokamak layer 2 tokenomics dubbed as “TON staking v2” 

## Background

The Tokamak Network is planning to launch an on-demand layer 2 (L2) platform that allows multiple L2s to thrive freely. However, without a proper economic system in place, this goal will remain a distant dream. Therefore, we will design tokenomics, with TON (the native token of the Tokamak Network) as a medium, that can facilitate the organic expansion of the Tokamak L2 ecosystem. As a first step towards this goal, relevant research is necessary to establish the theoretical foundation for the tokenomics, which we are calling "TON staking v2.”
